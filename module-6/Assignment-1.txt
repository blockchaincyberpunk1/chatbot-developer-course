Deep Learning Basics

Objective: Gain an understanding of the fundamentals of deep learning and its applications in Natural Language Processing (NLP).

In this assignment, you will delve into the core concepts of deep learning and explore its relevance in the field of Natural Language Processing. By the end of this assignment, you should have a solid grasp of the fundamental components of deep learning neural networks and how they are employed to address NLP tasks.

Instructions:

Part 1: Deep Learning Fundamentals:

Research and provide a detailed overview of deep learning concepts, including:
Neural networks and their architecture.
Different types of layers (e.g., input, hidden, output) used in neural networks.
Activation functions (e.g., ReLU, Sigmoid) and their significance.
The backpropagation algorithm for training neural networks.
Part 2: Applications in NLP:
2. Explain how deep learning is applied to Natural Language Processing tasks. Include discussions on:

Text classification: How deep learning models can classify text into categories or sentiments.
Named Entity Recognition (NER): How deep learning can extract entities (e.g., names, locations) from text.
Machine translation: The role of deep learning in neural machine translation models.
Sentiment analysis: How deep learning models can detect sentiment or emotion in text.
Part 3: Real-World Examples:
3. Provide real-world examples or case studies where deep learning has been successfully applied to NLP tasks. Discuss the specific challenges these applications addressed and the benefits they provided.

Part 4: Conclusion:
4. Summarize the key takeaways from your research and highlight the significance of deep learning in the context of NLP.

Part 5: References:
5. Include a list of credible sources and references to support your explanations and examples. Ensure proper citation formatting.

Grading Criteria:

Your assignment will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

Depth and accuracy of explanations regarding deep learning concepts.
Clarity in explaining the applications of deep learning in NLP.
Relevance and appropriateness of real-world examples.
